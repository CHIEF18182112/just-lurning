This is the Cinnamon Roll Recipe

Ingredients

Form the dough:

  • 1/2 cup worm milk
  • 2 1/2yeast
  • 1/2granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/2butter
  • 3 cup bread flour

For the felling

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 11/2 tablespoons ground Cinnamon

Instruction

  1. Mix the milk with yeast,sugar,egg.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. Add the flower and mix until combined into a dough.
  4. Knead the dough for 10 mnmutes.
  5. Transfer the dough into larg bowl and cover with plastic wrap.Leave it someware to rise for 2 hours.
